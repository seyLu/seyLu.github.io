I already had a background in computer science before I took the course cs50x from Harvard. C++, JavaScript, Java, and Python were among the first 4 programming languages that I've learned and familiarized with. And among these, C++ was my personal favourite, and my go-to language whenever I enter competitions.

After 4 years of coding, I had about another 4 years of not touching the keyboard, ever programming. A lot of it was due to my struggle with depression and just life in general, circumstances that are way out of my control.

Depression........

....... you know, .......it sucks man. I mean, no one wants to be depressed.

I got in, this really well known school in the country, and I was really hyped. But then after a month, the only thing that's constantly popping in my head is that I want to die.

So yeah, I dropped out. And got even more depressed in the process.

But that was months ago. And right now, I'm really thankful with the cs50 team.
